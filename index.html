Types of Third Party Insurance Coverage

At SD Loans and Leasing, we offer various types of third party insurance coverage to ensure you are fully protected:

  • Third Party Property Damage: Covers damage you cause to other people's property.
  • Third Party Fire and Theft: Covers damage to your vehicle caused by fire or theft, as well as third-party property damage.
  • Third Party Liability: Covers legal liabilities for injury or damage to third parties.

Benefits of Third Party Insurance

Third party insurance provides essential coverage, protecting you against a wide range of risks, including:
